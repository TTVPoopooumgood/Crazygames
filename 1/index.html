Helix Jump is a casual game with simple and addictively fun mechanics. In the game, you guide a bouncing ball continuously down a series of circular platforms until you crash. Fall through the cracks and avoid landing in the forbidden zones!

How To Play Helix Jump

The goal of Helix Jump is to guide a bouncing ball down through a series of rotating platforms without falling off the edge or hitting forbidden sections. The basic mechanics are easy to follow. The key is mastering your reflexes and looking ahead.

To guide the bouncing ball, rotate the platforms to align the ball with the cracks. Do this in quick succession for a combo move and higher points. If you go through 3 levels in quick succession, you're invincible for the first bounce after.

Note that Helix Jump features an endless number of levels with incremental difficulty, so you may want to pace yourself.

This is the official Helix Jump game developed by Voodoo. The game was originally released in 2018 on mobile devices and achieved viral success. You can now play this addictive game in your web browser for free at CrazyGames!
